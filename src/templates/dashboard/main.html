About Me

I'm Rayyan — a self-taught developer based in Pakistan, with 3+ years of experience working on real-world systems, internal business tools, and micro SaaS products.

My core strengths lie in backend development, system design, API creation, and automation. I'm most comfortable in Python (especially Django), Firebase, and Flutter. I’ve also worked with MySQL, PostgreSQL, REST APIs, WebSockets, and deployment platforms like Railway and Render.

I'm a strong problem solver, prefer clean and modular code, and like building things that actually work rather than just look pretty.

Outside of code, I'm usually experimenting with new ideas — from useful internal tools to fun, ambitious builds. I’m currently working on several personal projects, many of which are micro SaaS products that aren’t open-sourced yet. Alongside those, I’ve explored things like ethical malware concepts, a Jarvis-style AI assistant, and a bunch of other creative experiments.

Eventually, I plan to launch a blog or updates page to share what I’m working on in real-time. For now, this portfolio is just a glimpse into what I’ve done — and who I am.
